Frequently Asked Questions

It may be required depending on platform rules, supply type, turnover, and business structure. We check scope before filing.

Can I use a rented office for GST registration?

Yes, if the documents support the address and required NOC or proof is available.

Can you help after GST approval?

Yes. GST return filing, nil returns, LUT filing, annual return, cancellation, revocation, and notices are supported separately.
