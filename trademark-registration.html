What Is Trademark Registration?

A trademark is a legal protection for a brand identifier such as a name, logo, wordmark, tagline, product name, or service mark. Registration helps establish ownership and gives your business a stronger position if another party misuses a similar mark.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use TM after filing?

Businesses commonly start using TM after filing a trademark application. The registered symbol should be used only after registration is granted.

How long does trademark registration take?

Application filing can be quick once documents are ready. Final registration can take several months and may take longer if objection, opposition, or hearing steps arise.

Can I register both brand name and logo?

Yes. Depending on your brand strategy, you may file a wordmark, logo, or both. The right approach depends on how your brand is used.

What if my trademark receives an objection?

An objection does not always mean rejection. A reply may be filed with legal and factual reasoning. Hearing representation may be needed in some cases.
