Not Sure Which Service You Need?

Many business owners ask for one filing but actually need a different registration, correction, or compliance step first. These quick checks help us guide you faster.

If you are starting fresh

Share business activity, founders, state, capital plan, and whether you need GST, bank account, Startup India, or FSSAI after registration.

If you already have a business

Share entity type, GST status, last ROC or tax filing position, pending returns, and any deadline or portal issue.

If you received a notice

Share the notice copy, deadline, assessment or financial year, portal screenshot if available, and previous reply history.
