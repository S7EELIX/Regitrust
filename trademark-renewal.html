Frequently Asked Questions

When should a trademark be renewed?

Trademark renewal should be planned before expiry so the registration remains active without avoidable delay or restoration work.

Can an expired trademark be renewed?

Options may depend on timing, status, and applicable restoration provisions. Status should be checked quickly.

No. Final pricing depends on due date, delay, class count, owner details, and government fee.
