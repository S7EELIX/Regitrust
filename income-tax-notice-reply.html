Frequently Asked Questions

Can I ignore an income tax notice?

No. Ignoring a notice can lead to demand, penalties, refund delays, or further proceedings.

Can Regitrust help with AIS or Form 26AS mismatch?

Yes. Mismatches can be reviewed with income records, tax credits, bank entries, and filed return details.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on notice complexity, documents, urgency, and response or representation scope.
