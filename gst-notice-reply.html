No. Ignoring a GST notice can create larger compliance issues, penalties, or adverse orders.

Yes. Pending returns, reconciliations, and notices can be reviewed together to plan a practical response.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on notice complexity, records, urgency, and filing or hearing scope.
