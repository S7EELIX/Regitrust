Frequently Asked Questions

Can a person have more than one DIN?

No. A person should hold only one DIN. Duplicate DIN issues must be handled carefully through proper compliance steps.

Is DSC required for DIN?

DSC may be required depending on filing route and related MCA form requirements.

Is DIN enough to become a director?

No. DIN identifies the director, but appointment in a company requires separate corporate and MCA compliance.
