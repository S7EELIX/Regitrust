# Regitrust One-Campaign Ads Brief

## Campaign Goal

Generate higher-fit enquiries from NRIs, foreign founders, overseas companies, export-led businesses, SaaS teams, and serious growth businesses that need India company setup, GST, IEC, trademark, contracts, tax, or compliance planning.

## Landing Page Routing

Use one campaign, but split ad groups by intent:

- Foreign company / foreign founder intent:
  `https://regitrust.in/foreign-company-registration-india.html`

- NRI-specific intent:
  `https://regitrust.in/nri-company-registration-india.html`

- Broader India entry, IEC, GST, import-export, and operating setup:
  `https://regitrust.in/india-market-entry-services.html`

## Best-Fit Audience

- NRIs planning an India company
- Foreign founders entering India
- Overseas companies testing India operations
- SaaS, consulting, ecommerce, import-export, and export-led businesses
- Founders who need structure review before filing

## Avoid / Exclude

Avoid broad ads that attract low-fit local shop enquiries.

Negative keyword ideas:

- grocery
- kirana
- small shop
- free
- job
- salary
- loan
- ration
- local shop license
- labour job
- franchise
- cheapest
- near me only
- renewal only
- food stall
- street vendor

## Search Keyword Themes

Start narrow before expanding:

- NRI company registration in India
- foreign company registration in India
- foreign founder company registration India
- register foreign owned company in India
- India market entry services
- register company in India from abroad
- company registration for foreign founders India
- India subsidiary registration
- GST registration for foreign owned company India
- IEC registration for exporters India
- India business setup consultant
- private limited company registration for NRI

## Ad Message Direction

Use scope-first language:

- India company setup for NRIs and foreign founders
- Structure, documents, GST, IEC, and compliance roadmap
- Starts-from pricing after scope review
- Remote India setup coordination
- Avoid fixed-price promises before document review

## Example Headlines

- India Company Setup for NRIs
- Foreign Founder India Setup
- India Market Entry Support
- Register Company in India
- Remote India Setup Review
- GST, IEC and ROC Roadmap
- Scope First, File Clean

## Example Descriptions

- Plan India company setup with structure review, resident director planning, GST, IEC, and compliance roadmap.
- Support for NRIs, foreign founders, SaaS teams, exporters, and overseas companies entering India.
- Get a practical document checklist and starts-from quote after scope review.

## Tracking Notes

Use UTM parameters on every ad URL:

`?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=india_market_entry&utm_term={keyword}&utm_content={creative}`

The website stores UTM and GCLID data in lead submissions and fires `lead_confirmation_view` on the thank-you page after form submission.
