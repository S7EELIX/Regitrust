Frequently Asked Questions

Is GST registration mandatory for every business?

No. GST registration depends on turnover, business type, state, supply model, and specific legal requirements.

Can I sell on e-commerce platforms without GST?

Many e-commerce sellers require GST registration, though rules can vary based on product category and exemptions.

What if my GST application receives a query?

Regitrust can help prepare and submit clarification or additional documents based on the query raised.

What should I do after GST registration?

After registration, businesses must issue proper invoices, maintain records, file applicable returns, and pay tax where required.
