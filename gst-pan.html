Who Needs GST Registration

  • Businesses crossing the applicable turnover threshold
  • E-commerce sellers and marketplace vendors
  • Service providers operating across states
  • Import-export businesses
  • Businesses required to collect GST from customers
  • Companies needing GST for vendor, client, or tender onboarding

Documents Required

  • PAN of business or proprietor
  • Aadhaar details and mobile-linked authentication support
  • Business address proof
  • Bank details
  • Photograph
  • Authorization letter or board resolution, if applicable

Frequently Asked Questions

Is GST registration mandatory for every business?

No. GST registration depends on turnover, business type, state, supply model, e-commerce activity, and specific legal requirements.

Can I sell on e-commerce platforms without GST?

Many e-commerce sellers require GST registration, though rules can vary based on product category, platform requirements, and exemptions.

What if my GST application receives a query?

Regitrust can help prepare and submit clarification or additional documents based on the query raised by the department.

What should I do after GST registration?

After registration, businesses must issue proper invoices, maintain records, file applicable returns, and pay tax where required.

Is the starts-from fee the final GST registration cost?

No. The final quote depends on business type, documents, location count, urgency, query handling, and whether additional compliance support is required.
