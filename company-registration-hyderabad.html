FAQs

Can I register a Hyderabad company online?

The MCA filing process is online, but documents, DSC, signatures, address proof, GST needs, and compliance planning should be reviewed before filing.

Should a tech startup choose private limited company?

Private limited is commonly used by startups planning scale, equity, ESOPs, or investment, but LLP or OPC may suit some founder situations.
