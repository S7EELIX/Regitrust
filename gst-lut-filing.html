Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs GST LUT?

Exporters may use LUT to export without payment of IGST, subject to eligibility and GST compliance conditions.

Is LUT filed every year?

LUT is generally filed for the relevant financial year and should be managed before export transactions require it.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on GST status, pending returns, portal access, and export facts.
