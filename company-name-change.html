Frequently Asked Questions

Does company name change create a new company?

No. The legal entity generally remains the same; only the registered name changes after approval.

Is shareholder approval required?

Shareholder approval is generally required for changing a company name and related constitutional documents.

Should GST and PAN be updated after name change?

Yes. Tax, bank, license, contract, invoice, and business records should be reviewed after MCA approval.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on name review, company status, forms, government fees, urgency, and linked update work.
