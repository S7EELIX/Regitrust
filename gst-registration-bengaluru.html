Frequently Asked Questions

No. GST applicability depends on turnover, state, supply type, ecommerce platform rules, buyer requirements, and business activity.

How long does GST registration take?

Timeline depends on document quality, portal processing, and whether the department raises a query or clarification.

Can you also help with GST returns?

Yes. GST return filing, nil returns, late return cleanup, cancellation, revocation, and notice replies are supported separately.
