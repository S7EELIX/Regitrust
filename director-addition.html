Frequently Asked Questions

Is DIN mandatory for director appointment?

Yes. A person must generally have DIN before appointment as director in a company.

Does adding a director also make them a shareholder?

No. Director appointment and shareholding are separate matters unless shares are also issued or transferred.

Can Regitrust help if DIN is not available?

Yes. Regitrust can guide DIN and DSC requirements before the director appointment filing is prepared.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on documents, DIN/DSC status, filing scope, urgency, and whether additional board or MCA work is required.
