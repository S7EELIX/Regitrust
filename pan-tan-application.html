Frequently Asked Questions

Is TAN required for every business?

TAN is required when a person or business is responsible for deducting or collecting tax at source under applicable provisions.

Can PAN details be corrected?

Yes. Corrections may be filed with proper proof and applicant details, subject to authority processing.
