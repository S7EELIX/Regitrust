Frequently Asked Questions

Can a proprietorship apply for Startup India recognition?

DPIIT recognition is generally meant for eligible incorporated entities such as private limited companies, LLPs, or registered partnerships.

Does Startup India registration guarantee funding?

No. Recognition can improve eligibility for certain programs, but funding depends on separate investor, grant, or scheme criteria.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on eligibility review, document readiness, business summary preparation, and clarification scope.
