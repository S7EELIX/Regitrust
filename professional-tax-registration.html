Frequently Asked Questions

Is professional tax applicable in every state?

No. Professional tax is state-specific, and applicability depends on state law and business/employment facts.

Is professional tax different from income tax?

Yes. Professional tax is a state-level levy, while income tax is governed by central income tax law.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final quote depends on state, employee count, government fee, and compliance scope.
