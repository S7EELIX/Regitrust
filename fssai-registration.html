Frequently Asked Questions

Which FSSAI license do I need?

It depends on turnover, food activity, location count, manufacturing capacity, and whether the business is local, state-level, or central-level in scope.

Can a home food business apply for FSSAI?

Yes. Many home food businesses can apply, but the correct category depends on turnover, activity, and local compliance requirements.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on license category, government fees, documents, location count, inspection needs, and query handling.
