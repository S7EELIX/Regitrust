Frequently Asked Questions

How many partners are required for LLP registration?

An LLP generally requires at least two partners, and at least two designated partners for compliance and filing responsibilities.

Is LLP better than private limited company?

It depends on funding plans, ownership needs, compliance budget, and business model. LLP can suit professional and closely-held businesses, while private limited companies are often preferred for equity funding.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. The final quote depends on partner count, DSC, state, agreement scope, documents, and additional registrations required.
