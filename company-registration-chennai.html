FAQs

Is Chennai company registration different from other cities?

The MCA incorporation process is national, but address proof, GST, local registrations, and business activity needs can vary by case.

Can I add IEC after company registration?

Yes. IEC can be planned after incorporation where import or export activity is relevant.
