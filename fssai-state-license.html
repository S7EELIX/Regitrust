Who Should Consider State License

  • Restaurants, cafes, cloud kitchens, and caterers with state-level operations
  • Food manufacturers, processors, packers, and relabellers
  • Food traders, distributors, and storage operators
  • Businesses that have outgrown basic FSSAI registration
  • Food businesses needing stronger compliance for vendors or platforms

State, Central, or Renewal?

If your business operates across states, imports food, or falls into a larger regulated category, review FSSAI Central License. If you already hold a license and need validity extension, use FSSAI License Renewal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How is State License different from Basic Registration?

State License generally applies to larger or more regulated food activities than basic registration, depending on turnover, capacity, and business type.

Can one state license cover multiple states?

Usually no. Multi-state or larger operations may need central license or separate compliance review.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final quote depends on activity, products, premises, government fee, inspection, and query scope.
