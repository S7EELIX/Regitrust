Privacy Policy

Last updated: 23 June 2026

Regitrust Services LLP respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our website, submit an enquiry, or communicate with us for registration, GST, compliance, and legal documentation services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, city or state, service requirement, business stage, timeline or urgency, preferred contact method, business details, documents shared for service execution, and messages submitted through our forms, WhatsApp, email, or calls.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to respond to enquiries, provide consultations, prepare service proposals, process assignments, maintain client records, send service updates, and comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share relevant information with government portals, professional partners, payment processors, form/email providers, or advisors only when needed to deliver requested services or meet legal requirements.

4. Enquiry Storage and Communication Tools

Website form enquiries may be sent to our email inbox through a form submission provider and may also be copied to an internal lead sheet or similar business tool so our team can track callbacks, service requests, urgency, and follow-up status. WhatsApp, email, phone, analytics, hosting, and spreadsheet providers may process limited information as part of normal enquiry handling.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect information. No online system is completely risk-free, so please avoid submitting highly sensitive documents unless requested through an appropriate communication channel.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies, analytics, or third-party tools to understand site performance and enquiry activity. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Your Choices

You may ask us to update, correct, or delete enquiry information where legally permissible. Some records may be retained when required for service, tax, accounting, or regulatory reasons.

8. Contact

For privacy requests, email contact@regitrust.in or call +91-8984297666 / +91-6306898090.
