Frequently Asked Questions

Can software be copyrighted?

Yes. Software may be protected as literary work, subject to proper application and required material.

Does copyright protect a brand name?

No. Brand names are usually protected through trademark registration, not copyright.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on work type, ownership documents, government fee, and objection scope.
