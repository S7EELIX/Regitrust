Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Bengaluru business register a trademark for all India?

Yes. Indian trademark registration is national, so a Bengaluru business can apply for brand protection across India.

Should I file a word mark or logo?

It depends on your brand use. Many businesses prioritize the word mark first because the name usually matters more than one logo version.

What if a trademark objection comes?

An objection is not automatic rejection. It needs timely review, reply planning, and supporting documents or arguments.
