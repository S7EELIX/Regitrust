Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Chennai business register a trademark for all India?

Yes. Indian trademark registration is national, so Chennai businesses can apply for protection across India.

Is trademark useful for export businesses?

Yes. Trademark registration helps protect the brand identity used on products, packaging, websites, and marketplace listings.

What if a trademark objection comes?

An objection needs a timely reply. Regitrust can help review grounds, documents, usage proof, and next steps.
