Who We Are

Regitrust Services LLP is a professional services firm helping entrepreneurs and businesses with registrations, GST, ROC filings, tax support, legal documentation, intellectual property, and recurring compliance.

Our work is built around one simple idea: clients should understand what they are filing, why it matters, what documents are needed, how pricing is decided, and what comes after completion.

How We Work

  1. Requirement review: We understand your business stage, location, entity plan, and immediate filing need.
  2. Route confirmation: We confirm the right filing path and what documents are needed before work starts.
  3. Scope-based quote: We share starts-from pricing first and final quote after scope, urgency, and document review.
  4. Execution: We prepare filing data, documents, drafts, portal details, or response points.
  5. After-filing clarity: We explain the next compliance step after registration, acknowledgement, or filing completion.

How We Protect Client Confidence

  • Confidential handling: Business details, founder documents, and filing information are treated as sensitive client information.
  • No forced transparent pricing: Final pricing depends on the exact scope, so clients are not pushed into a misleading one-size package.
  • Remote-friendly process: WhatsApp, phone, email, and online document coordination make support practical across India and abroad.
  • Practical language: We explain filings and compliance duties in business terms, not only portal or legal jargon.
