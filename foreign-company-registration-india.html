Common Questions

Can a foreign founder register a company in India?

Yes, subject to structure, documents, resident director requirements, sector rules, banking readiness, and applicable compliance checks.

Is a resident director required?

Many Indian company routes require at least one resident director. The right arrangement should be reviewed before documents are prepared.

Should GST and IEC be planned before incorporation?

Usually yes. GST, IEC, invoices, contracts, banking, and first compliance dates can affect the structure and launch timeline.
