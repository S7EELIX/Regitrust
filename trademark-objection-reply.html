Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if objection reply is not filed?

The application may be treated as abandoned if required response is not filed within the applicable timeline.

Does filing reply guarantee registration?

No. Reply filing keeps the application moving, but acceptance depends on registry review, objection grounds, evidence, and hearing outcome if any.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on objection complexity, evidence, urgency, and hearing or follow-up scope.
