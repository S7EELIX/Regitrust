Who Needs ROC Filing Support

  • Hyderabad private limited companies completing yearly compliance
  • IT services, startups, agencies, ecommerce, restaurant, and consulting companies

  • Businesses preparing for funding, loans, tenders, or due diligence

Documents Required

  • Audited financial statements
  • Board report and AGM details, where applicable
  • Director and shareholder details
  • DSC of authorised director
  • Previous filing records and MCA master data
  • Auditor and statutory information

Process

  1. Review company status, due dates, and pending filings
  2. Collect financial statements and statutory information
  3. Prepare applicable forms such as AOC-4 and MGT-7 or MGT-7A
  4. File forms with MCA using DSC and required certifications
  5. Download challans and maintain filing acknowledgements
  6. Share next annual compliance calendar
