Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Hong Kong or Singapore company set up an Indian company?

Yes, but the correct route depends on ownership, sector, resident director needs, documents, signing process, registered office, tax, and banking readiness.

Can Regitrust help before incorporation?

Yes. The scope review can cover entity route, documents, GST, IEC, contracts, trademark, accounting, and first-year compliance before filing starts.

Do you give fixed pricing immediately?

We share starts-from pricing after reviewing scope because foreign ownership, notarization, apostille, DSC, GST, IEC, and urgency can change the work.

Is this page for small local shop registration?

No. Basic local shop registrations and lowest-fee one-time filings are lower priority. This funnel is for serious cross-border India setup.
