Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Hyderabad business register a trademark for all India?

Yes. Indian trademark registration is national, so a Hyderabad business can apply for protection across India.

Is trademark useful for service businesses?

Yes. Service businesses can protect their business name, productized service name, software name, logo, or tagline.

What if a trademark objection comes?

An objection should be reviewed quickly. Reply strategy depends on objection grounds, brand usage, evidence, and class details.
