Frequently Asked Questions

Can GST registration be cancelled voluntarily?

Yes, eligible taxpayers may apply for cancellation when registration is no longer required, subject to compliance status and department review.

Do pending GST returns affect cancellation?

Yes. Pending returns, notices, or liabilities can delay or complicate cancellation.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final quote depends on pending filings, notices, final return, and GST portal status.
