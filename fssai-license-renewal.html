Frequently Asked Questions

When should FSSAI renewal be started?

Renewal should be planned before expiry so the food business avoids avoidable delay, penalty, or interruption risk.

Can an expired FSSAI license be renewed?

Options depend on expiry status, portal rules, delay, and license category. The status should be checked quickly.

Is renewal different from modification?

Yes. Renewal extends validity, while modification updates details such as address, activity, product category, or business information.
