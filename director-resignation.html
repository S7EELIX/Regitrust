Frequently Asked Questions

Can a director resign without company cooperation?

The correct route depends on facts, documents, and MCA record position. The resignation communication and filing status should be reviewed carefully.

Is a resigning director responsible after resignation?

Liability depends on facts, default period, and statutory records. Proper documentation and filing help establish the resignation record.

What if the company will have fewer than the required directors?

A replacement or additional appointment may need to be planned so the company remains compliant after resignation.

Is the starts-from fee final?

No. Final pricing depends on documents, filing delay, DSC status, company records, and whether linked director addition or compliance cleanup is required.
