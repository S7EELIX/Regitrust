Frequently Asked Questions

Can a Lucknow business register a trademark for all India?

Yes. Indian trademark registration is not limited to one city; a Lucknow business can apply for brand protection across India.

Should I register word mark or logo?

It depends on your brand use and protection priority. Many businesses start with the word mark because the brand name usually matters more than one logo version.

What if an objection comes?

An objection does not automatically mean rejection. It must be reviewed, answered within timeline, and supported with appropriate facts and documents.
