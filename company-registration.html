Frequently Asked Questions

How many directors are required for a private limited company?

A private limited company generally requires at least two directors and two shareholders. The same people can act as both directors and shareholders.

Can NRIs register a private limited company in India?

Yes. NRIs and foreign nationals can participate, subject to required documents, identity verification, and applicable FEMA or RBI compliance.

Do I need an office before registration?

You need a registered office address. It may be owned, rented, or otherwise legally available with proper proof and NOC where required.

What happens after incorporation?

After incorporation, the company must maintain books, complete statutory filings, handle tax registrations where required, and comply with annual ROC requirements.

Is the "starts from" fee the final cost?

No. The final quote depends on your structure, state, number of directors, DSC requirements, capital, document readiness, and any additional compliance or registration work required.

Can Regitrust help after the company is registered?

Yes. Regitrust can help with GST registration, accounting, ROC filing, tax compliance, legal documentation, trademark registration, and recurring compliance planning.
